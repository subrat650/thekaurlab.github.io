The Kaur Lab

Computational Biophysics & Photosynthesis Research

About Us

The Kaur Computational Lab focuses on understanding the structure, dynamics, and function of biological systems using molecular dynamics simulations, quantum chemistry, and multiscale modelling approaches. Our research explores photosynthetic complexes, interfacial water dynamics, and molecular mechanisms in biological energy conversion.

Our Research

Photosynthetic Complexes

Investigating structure–function relationships in Photosystem I and Photosystem II systems using computational approaches.

Molecular Dynamics

Studying protein, water, and membrane dynamics through atomistic molecular dynamics simulations.

QM/MM & Multiscale Modeling

Exploring electronic structures, energy transfer mechanisms, and catalytic processes using multiscale methods.
