The Kaur Lab

Computational Biophysics & Photosynthesis Research

The Kaur Lab focuses on understanding the structure, dynamics, and function of biological systems using molecular dynamics simulations, quantum chemistry, and multiscale modeling approaches, with particular emphasis on photosynthetic complexes, interfacial water, and energy conversion processes.
