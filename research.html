Research

Scientific themes and ongoing projects

Photosynthetic Systems

Computational investigation of Photosystem I and II, including pigment–protein interactions, energy transfer, and redox processes.

Interfacial Water

Structure and dynamics of hydration water near proteins and cofactors, and its role in stability, function, and energy landscapes.

Molecular Dynamics Simulations

Classical MD simulations of biomolecular systems using AMBER and related tools to understand conformational dynamics.

QM/MM & Electronic Structure

Hybrid QM/MM calculations to study electronic properties, charge transfer, and reaction mechanisms in complex biomolecules.

Multiscale Modeling

Bridging atomistic and coarse-grained approaches to capture biological processes across length and time scales.
