Prof. Divya Kaur Matta

Assistant Professor, Chemistry

The Kaur Lab is led by Prof. Divya Kaur Matta, focusing on computational biophysics, photosynthetic systems, interfacial water, and molecular simulations. The lab combines molecular dynamics, quantum chemistry, and multiscale modelling to understand biological energy conversion.
